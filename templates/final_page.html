Where to visit Dinosaurs

Dinosuar bones have been found all over the world and there are lots of places you can visit their skeletons. Some places have put the skeletons back together, while other have built replicas and some have even built animatronics (moving models). Below are some links for places you can visit to see what's left of these pre-historic wonders

The Natural History Museum London
Dublin Zoo
Dinosaur Live at Tayto Park
The Dinosaur Museum Dorchester
